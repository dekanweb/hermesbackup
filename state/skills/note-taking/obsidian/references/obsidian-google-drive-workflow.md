# Obsidian + Google Drive workflow

Use this when the user treats Obsidian and Google Drive as complementary systems.

## Guiding split
- **Obsidian vault**: long-form notes, structure, backlinks, planning, daily capture, idea processing.
- **Google Drive**: source documents, PDFs, scans, spreadsheets, exports, shareable business files.

## Practical workflow
1. Capture quick notes into the Obsidian Inbox.
2. Store source files in Drive when they are documents, scans, or files the user may share.
3. Extract text from PDFs/scans with `ocr-and-documents` before moving the useful content into Obsidian.
4. Link between systems by using clear filenames and Drive links inside notes.
5. Keep the Obsidian vault lightweight; do not duplicate everything from Drive unless the user explicitly wants a mirror.
